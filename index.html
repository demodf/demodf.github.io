About Us

Welcome to RoomHunting Officer Page

People nowadays are leaving their own home to pursue their studies or work. Often, they will face problem in finding accommodation that suits their condition especially in price and strategic places.

They will need to spend extra time researching accommodation all over the internet to find the best suits. Each time, they find information from a website, they are required to register they personal information. Just imagine, if they are interested in ten shortlisted accommodation from different website, they will need to register for multiple times with the same information. It will be a wasted of time and messy. However, if we provide a system that centralized all the available and affordable accommodation, it will save the applicant time where they just need to register their information for one time. Applicant can also easily update their personal information.
